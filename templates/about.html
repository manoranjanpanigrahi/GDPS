This is a Disease Prediction System with the ability to predict Heart Disease, Diabetes and Parkinson with an accuracy of 90%, 77% and 87 respectively. It is developed by a group of Students from University of Engineering & Management, Kolkata under the guidance of Mr. Kaustav Bhattacharjee (HoD, Computer Application Department).
The Project uses various Machine Learning Models to take the inputs from the user and predicts and outome accordingly.
This projects aims to be strictly used for reference purposes only.
